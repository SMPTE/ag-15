Purpose

This Administrative Guideline sets forth the participation agreement for guests in attendance at meetings of the SMPTE Standards Community.

Definitions

Society of Motion Picture and Television Engineers is hereinafter “SMPTE” or “the Society”

Participation Agreement

The participation agreement (GuestSOP-v1-2014-10-30.pdf) shall be signed by all guests at meetings of Technology Committees or their subgroups. Completed forms shall be sent to the Director of Engineering (standards@smpte.org).
